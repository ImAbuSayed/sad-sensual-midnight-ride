[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers burning in the dark
Every time you leave a little mark
Drawing me closer with a gentle hand
Making me stay right where I stand

[Verse 1]
The stars are fading in the dusty sky
I see the lingering question in your eye
We left the comfort of the glowing town
Just to feel the way the night comes down

[Pre-Chorus]
And the silence speaks in the things we do
I am pulling on the gravity of you
There is a gentle ache in the way we touch
Never thought that I could want so much

[Chorus]
Take me away on this midnight ride
Nowhere to go and nowhere to hide
I need the warmth of your skin on mine
Holding you close till the end of time

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] (Take my breath away)
Pulling me closer to you now
